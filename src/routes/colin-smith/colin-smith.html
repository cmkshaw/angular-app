Colin Smith Woodworking

I built a WordPress theme for Colin's new website in order to support his freelance carpentry business in Toronto.

With formal UX recommendations, the UX designer and I collaborated on the overall architecture for Colin's new wesite. Using Roots.io as a base theme, custom post types were leveraged with Advanced Custom Fields to create an extensible CMS for future products.
